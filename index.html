Opportunties and Risks of Artifical Intelligence in Society

Summary

Nadimpalli (2017) puts into conclusion that, artificial intelligence has its benefits and risks. Society can benefit both from artificial intelligence as well as potentially suffer from it. The fact that artificial intelligence could create some of the best outcomes and produce a high-quality product is very significant. Artificial intelligence especially has tremendous benefits in the field of medical or health, education, transportation, as well as enhancing human activities, and markets. On the other hand, artificial intelligence has the overall misconceptions and risks of replacing human beings as well as reducing their emotional aspects, observing them as machines as opposed to human beings.

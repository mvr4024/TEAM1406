Ethical issues relating to artificial intelligence

The development and improvement of artificial intelligence systems comes out with the idea that machines should have responsibility, which therefore, should have ethical attributes. In addition, these developments of artificial intelligence systems should have ethical features when used to make decisions such as scenario judgement and analysis. Currently, the implementation of ethics into artificial intelligence has become the key research of its development (Chao, 2019). Ethics of artificial intelligence focuses on the importance of behaviors that machines bring to other machines, and especially towards human decisions (Chao, 2019). One of the primary examples is the application of self-driving vehicles, this artificial intelligence machine has to be able to make ethical decisions. According to Baum (2019, as cited in Chao, 2019), one of the major ethical issues of artificial intelligence is the use of social choice by the intelligent systems, in which the artificial intelligence is implemented or designed to operate based on the collective views of the society. There is no particular moral ethics in society therefore, the basis of artificial intelligence social ethics choice is fragile and unsteady because of the fact that there is no combined one point of view from the society regarding ethics. The proposal design of artificial intelligence should include three things: standing, regarding whose ethics views are compromised; measurement, the identification of their views towards the society; and aggregation, how the united views of the society can be made into a single view that can model and guide artificial intelligence performance. The design of artificial intelligence should be implemented through pre-defined decisions. All the questions are more significant than debatable decision of using the society to choose ethical problems. The key point should be on these issues, and not on the choice of social decisions. The algorithm of ethics towards artificial intelligence should be the decision-making acts of these autonomous systems. These systems have to be reliable and secure. The artificial intelligence of these devices or machines should be safe towards the society, it should be able to make moral decisions better than human beings. Researchers, designers, and engineers of artificial intelligence or the society should clarify how these machines or vehicles make moral decisions that is morality of artificial intelligence.
